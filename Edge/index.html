About Us

Mission:
We facilitate and encourage a cooperative team environment and collaboration that is necessary to provide excellent service and believe that every project is an opportunity to develop a long-term relationship with our clients building a sustainable future.

Vision:
To be known by all professionals as the best actuarial consulting firm in the industry by providing high levels of customer service and quality work with  innovative solutions that will revolutionize the way we  sustain.

Our company serves in the field of applied research and product development and manufacturing tailored to automotive industry. It undertakes contract based research and project from industries and also manufactures customised and non-customised products for industries and people working in automotive sector. The company partners with the institutions like universities and non-universities organizations to avail their resources for doing research and project works and developing products with the direct involvement of promising students, faculties and research scholars from various institutions. It ,therefore, presents students and faculties with opportunities to work on research and product development project, at the same time takes institutions into the mainstream of collaborative work with industries. In this scenario, these industries of automotive sector and the people involved in different automotive related projects are potential customers of the company.

Services

Edge Automotive consultancy is one of the pioneers in the field of applied research and product development in automotive sectors in India. The company undertakes research project from industries and also manufactures both customised and non-customised products for industries and people doing automobile related project work. The research and manufacturing work leverages the resources of various partner institutions.
